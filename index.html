Music

I play a lot of different instruments. I started out playing trumpet in elementary school. then in high school I picked up a guitar and never looked back. In college I started composing electronic music and had to start learning piano. I can play bass, but I play bass like a guitarist. I love to experiment with sound, hence I own many effects pedals. I play guitar with a cello bow, ebow, slide, trem picking, tapping, drumsticks, whatever I can think of I try. Life and art are about creation, experimentation, and pushing boundaries.
